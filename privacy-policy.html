Privacy Policy for SideWriter QuickDocs

Last Updated: April 29, 2025

1. Introduction

SideWriter QuickDocs ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our Chrome extension.

2. Information We Collect

  • Google Account Access: We request the minimum necessary permissions:
    • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.readonly
    • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/documents
  • Document Content: Only processed to fulfill the extension's functionality.
  • Local Storage: Used for storing preferences, never transmitted.
  • Usage Data: Collected anonymously for improvements.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To enable extension functionality.
  • All document interaction happens via official Google APIs.
  • No mining or unauthorized analysis of document data.

4. Data Storage and Retention

We do not store document data. OAuth tokens are managed securely by Chrome's Identity API.

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

No data is sold, rented, or shared. Data exchange only occurs via Google's APIs.

6. Data Security

  • All API calls use HTTPS.
  • Official OAuth 2.0 is used for authentication.
  • Follows Google’s Chrome extension security guidelines.
  • CSP is implemented to prevent code injection attacks.

7. Your Data Rights

  • You can revoke access via Google Account Permissions.
  • Uninstall the extension to delete local data.
  • Contact us for any privacy-related concerns.

8. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. Updates will appear here with a revised date.

10. Third-Party Services

Google services are used. Your use is subject to Google’s terms and privacy policy.

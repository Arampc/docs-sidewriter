Privacy Policy for SideWriter QuickDocs

Last Updated: May 6, 2025

1. Introduction

SideWriter QuickDocs ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our Chrome extension.

2. Google User Data We Access

Our extension requires access to specific Google user data to function properly:

Google Drive Read Access (https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.readonly):

  • We access the names and IDs of your Google Docs files to display them in our document selector.
  • This allows you to easily choose which document to add text to without manually entering document IDs.
  • We do not access or read the content of documents until you specifically select them.

Google Docs Modification Access (https://www.googleapis.com/auth/documents):

  • We access the content of Google Docs files you specifically select to:
  • Generate a preview of the document content in our extension interface
  • Add formatted text that you create in our extension to your selected document
  • Apply formatting including styles, colors, lists, and other text formatting you create

3. How We Use Your Google User Data

  • Document Lists: Your Google Docs file names and IDs are temporarily loaded in the extension interface to enable document selection.
  • Document Content: Content from your selected document is accessed only to:
    • Generate a preview within the extension interface
    • Determine where to add your new content
    • Apply formatted text that you create within the extension
  • No Data Storage: We do not store, retain, or transmit your document data to any external servers. All processing happens locally in your browser.
  • No Data Mining: We do not analyze, mine, or use your document content for any purpose other than the direct functionality of the extension.

4. Information We Collect

  • Local Storage: Used only for storing your preferences within the extension, never transmitted to external servers.
  • Usage Data: Basic anonymous usage statistics may be collected to improve the extension.

5. Data Storage and Retention

We do not store your Google Docs or Drive data on our servers. All document interaction happens directly between your browser and Google's APIs. OAuth tokens are managed securely by Chrome's Identity API and stored only in your browser.

6. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties. The only data exchange occurs directly between your browser and Google's APIs when you use the extension.

7. Data Security

  • All API calls use HTTPS encryption.
  • We use Google's official OAuth 2.0 authentication system.
  • The extension follows Google's Chrome extension security guidelines.
  • Content Security Policy (CSP) is implemented to prevent code injection attacks.
  • Your documents are accessed only when you explicitly interact with the extension.

8. Your Data Rights

  • You can revoke access to your Google data at any time via Google Account Permissions.
  • Uninstalling the extension will remove all local data stored by the extension.
  • You can request information about your data by contacting us at [YOUR CONTACT EMAIL].

9. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 years of age.

Privacy Policy for QuickDocs: Write Faster in Google Docs

Last Updated: April 29, 2025

1. Introduction

QuickDocs: Write Faster in Google Docs ("we", "our", "us", or "the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our Chrome extension.

2. Information We Collect

  • Google Account Access: The Extension requires you to grant permission to access your Google Drive and Google Docs through Google's OAuth authentication system. We request only the minimum permissions necessary:
    • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.readonly - To allow you to see and select from your Google Docs files
    • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/documents - To enable adding text to your selected Google Docs
  • Document Content: When you use the Extension to add text to a document, we process the content you input and the document preview information. This data is processed only to fulfill the functionality of the Extension.
  • Local Storage: The Extension may store your formatting preferences locally on your device to improve your user experience. This data never leaves your device.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage statistics to understand how the Extension is being used and to identify areas for improvement.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Providing and Improving the Service: We use the access you grant to display your Google Docs files and add text content to your selected documents.
  • Document Interaction: All document interactions occur through official Google APIs, and we only perform the specific actions you initiate.
  • Service Enhancement: Usage statistics help us understand which features are most valuable and where improvements are needed.
  • No Data Mining: We do not analyze, mine, or extract information from your documents for any purpose beyond providing the Extension's core functionality.

4. Data Storage and Retention

The Extension does not maintain a database of your documents or their contents. Document data is only accessed during the specific operations you initiate and is not stored on our servers. Your OAuth token is managed securely by Chrome's identity API and is not accessible to us.

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share any information collected by the Extension with third parties. The only data exchange occurs between the Extension and Google's servers through their official APIs, which is necessary for the Extension to function.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • All communication with Google APIs uses secure HTTPS connections
  • We use Google's official OAuth 2.0 implementation for secure authentication
  • The Extension follows Google's security best practices for Chrome extensions
  • Content Security Policy (CSP) is implemented to prevent cross-site scripting and other code injection attacks

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Data Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access Control: You can revoke the Extension's access to your Google account at any time by visiting Google Account Permissions.
  • Data Deletion: Since we don't store your data on our servers, there is no personal data to delete beyond what is managed by Google. To revoke access and clear local storage, you can uninstall the Extension.
  • Transparency: You can contact us at any time with questions about your data and how it's being used.

8. Children's Privacy

The Extension is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe we may have collected information from your child, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

10. Third-Party Services

The Extension integrates with Google services through their APIs. Your use of Google services is subject to Google's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review those policies.
